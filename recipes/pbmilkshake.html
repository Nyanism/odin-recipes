Peanut Butter Milkshake

Description

The undisputed king of milkshakes - well, according to me at least - is the peanut butter milkshake. In the name of balance, we have gone from a light salad, all the way to a milkshake that might worry your nutritionist. I would, however, guarantee that your nutritionist will go nuts over this milkshake at the end of the day as well.

The drink has only a few ingredients, but I am certain that they will combine together to form a tasty treat. As with all milkshakes out there, feel free to add any toppings you wish!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Chopped peanuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients for a nice photo, so that you would be able to tell your Instagram followers all about this wonderful milkshake.
  2. Place the ice cream, whole milk, and peanut butter into your blender.
  3. Blend until completely blended and smooth.
  4. Pour the shakes into 2 large glasses, then top it off with the whipped cream and chopped peanuts. Any other toppings that you desire should go on top too!

And that's it!You're done!
